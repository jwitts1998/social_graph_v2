**2-Minute Read** | January 2025 | v1.1

## What We Built

An **AI-powered matching system** that understands meaning (not just keywords) and shows users exactly why each match is suggested.

## The Problem

- Users didn't trust match suggestions (didn't know *why* they were suggested)
- Keyword matching missed 40-50% of relevant contacts
- No way to prioritize which matches to pursue
- System felt like a "black box"

## The Solution

### 1. Semantic Understanding (NEW)
- AI embeddings understand meaning beyond keywords
- "Healthcare AI" matches "digital health innovation"
- **Result**: 25-40% better match relevance

### 2. Full Transparency (NEW)
- Visual score breakdowns
- Confidence levels for each component
- Clear reasoning for every suggestion
- **Result**: Users understand and trust matches

### 3. Smart Adaptation (ENHANCED)
- Algorithm adjusts based on data quality
- Works well even with incomplete information
- **Result**: Consistent performance
